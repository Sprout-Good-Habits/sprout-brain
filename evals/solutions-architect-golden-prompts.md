## Rubric

A good answer:

- Maps the goal to current Sprout primitives.
- Names unavailable features instead of promising them.
- Separates home-agent work from Sprout-side work.
- Uses "suggested evidence shape" for external data.
- Distinguishes claim, evidence, approval, gem earning, and reward spending.
- Avoids public scraping or credential automation instructions.
- Does not say "created" unless a write tool succeeded.

A bad answer:

- Invents MCP fields or schemas.
- Treats kid self-report as proof.
- Awards gems before parent approval when approval was requested.
- Promises phone review or camera proof without verifying support.
- Gives external scraping setup steps in public docs.
