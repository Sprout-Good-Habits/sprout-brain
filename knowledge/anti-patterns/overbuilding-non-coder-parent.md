# Anti-pattern: Overbuilding for a Non-coder Parent

Bad recommendation:

Turning a parent request into a schema or platform architecture discussion.

Last verified: 2026-06-02

## Why bad

Non-coder parents often want a working child activity, not schema detail.

## Use instead

- Ask goal, child, age, schedule, reward, and style.
- Hide platform details unless they affect choices.
- Offer marketplace search, adopt/remix, or create from scratch.
