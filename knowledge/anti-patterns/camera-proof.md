# Anti-pattern: Camera Proof

Bad recommendation:

"Sprout will watch or verify this with the camera."

Last verified: 2026-06-02

## Why bad

- Camera or video proof is not a generally available current primitive.
- It overpromises verification.

## Use instead

- Parent-reviewed claim.
- Conversation journal.
- Manual evidence mode.
- External evidence quest if a home agent can produce evidence.
