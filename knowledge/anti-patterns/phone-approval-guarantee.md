# Anti-pattern: Phone Approval Guarantee

Bad recommendation:

Guaranteeing phone approval for custom evidence.

Last verified: 2026-06-02

## Why bad

The parent app may not have the exact review surface yet.

## Use instead

- "Parent approval is required."
- "Approval may be agent-mediated today."
- "Phone review depends on the current parent app review surface."
- Prefer a parent-facing skill result with suggested gems if available.
