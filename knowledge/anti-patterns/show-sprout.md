# Anti-pattern: Show Sprout

Bad recommendation:

"Show Sprout your work and Sprout will know."

Last verified: 2026-06-02

## Why bad

Unless a specific input surface exists, Sprout cannot inspect arbitrary
real-world objects or actions.

## Use instead

- "Tell Sprout what you did."
- "Ask your parent to check."
- "Upload or enter evidence if the current product supports that input."
