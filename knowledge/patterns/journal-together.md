# Pattern: Journal Together

Use when the child should reflect, explain, or build language around an
experience.

Last verified: 2026-06-02

## Current Sprout shape

- `task_create` with `runMode: "conversation"`.
- `conversationSpec.goalType` usually `share`, `explain`, or `practice`.

## Examples

- "Tell Sprout what you practiced in Khan today."
- "What was one hard thing and one thing you figured out?"
- "Explain multiplication by 7 like you are teaching a younger kid."

## Reward rule

- If the journal is the activity, small completion rewards can be okay.
- If the journal is a claim about outside work, require parent review before
  gems.
