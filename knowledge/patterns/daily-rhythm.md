# Pattern: Daily Rhythm

Use when a parent wants a day, week, or summer plan.

Last verified: 2026-06-02

## Current Sprout shape

- Scheduled tasks for predictable routines.
- Conversation tasks for journaling and reflection.
- Canvas tasks for focused practice or exploration.
- Parent-reviewed rewards for motivation.

## Good first plan

- Morning launch: short conversation or checklist.
- Learning quest: 10-15 minute task or canvas.
- Offline activity: parent-reviewed claim.
- Reflection: "Sprout and kid journal together."
- Reward check: show progress toward a parent-defined reward.

## Avoid

- Camera-based verification unless available.
- Tasks that say "show Sprout" an object or action when Sprout cannot inspect
  it.
- Huge all-day plans that require custom surfaces for every block.
