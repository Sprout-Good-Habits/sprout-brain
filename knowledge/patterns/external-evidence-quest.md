# Pattern: External Evidence Quest

Use when a third-party product has parent-visible progress and the child should
be motivated by missions or rewards.

Last verified: 2026-06-02

## Examples

- Khan Academy progress.
- Duolingo practice.
- Reading app sessions.
- School platform assignments.

## Current Sprout shape

- Home agent produces evidence or parent enters it manually.
- Sprout renders a mission canvas or conversation task.
- Parent-facing result summarizes evidence and suggested reward.
- Parent approval grants gems.
- Reward catalog tracks spending goals.

## Suggested evidence shape should answer

- What changed?
- Which child?
- When was it observed?
- What subject or skill?
- Was there a score, duration, or milestone?
- What key prevents duplicate reward?

## Avoid

- Public scraping instructions.
- Credentials stored in Sprout.
- Kid click treated as external evidence.
- "Khan/Duolingo/Amazon integration" wording that implies official partnership
  unless one exists.
