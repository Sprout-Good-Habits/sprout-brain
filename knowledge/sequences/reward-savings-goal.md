# Sequence: Reward Savings Goal

Use when the child is working toward a concrete item.

Last verified: 2026-06-02

## Steps

1. `reward_create` for the parent-defined reward.
2. Assign the reward to the child with the desired gem requirement.
3. Earn gems through tasks or reviews.
4. Child claims the reward when enough gems are available.
5. Parent approves or denies the reward claim.

## Example

- Reward: "Switch game"
- Cost: 50 gems
- Earning path: reviewed Khan evidence
