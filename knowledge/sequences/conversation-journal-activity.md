# Sequence: Conversation or Journal Activity

Use when the goal is reflection, explanation, coaching, or simple practice.

Last verified: 2026-06-02

## Steps

1. `family_query_overview`.
2. `skill_write` for the chat-output skill when a governing skill is needed.
3. `task_create` with `runMode: "conversation"` and concrete
   `conversationSpec.guidance`.

## Good examples

- "Sprout and the child journal about what was hard in learning practice today."
- "Explain one multiplication trick you learned."
- "Practice asking for help when stuck."
